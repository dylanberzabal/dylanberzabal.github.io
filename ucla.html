UCLA Athletics

As a graphic designer in UCLA’s Athletic Department, I create various digital and physical designs to promote the current sports season as well as UCLA’s rich athletic history. I have spearheaded various major display cases in our Hall of Fame, which I researched, designed, and installed.

Major projects I’ve completed include the UCLA Fans Case (history of UCLA fans), Welcome to Los Angeles Case (guide to Westwood and LA), and the Bruins Everywhere Case (UCLA alumni).








UCA: Undergraduate Communication Association

The Undergraduate Communication Association (UCA) is a student-run organization that promotes interest and success in the communications field, helping students develop their network as well as assisting in professional growth through workshops and networking nights.

As Director of Design, I create print and digital graphics to promote UCA on campus and online. These designs include banners, social media posts, merchandise, speaker plaques, flyers, and more.
