su/per/cut: An Immersive Short Film

A nighttime drive through ones hometown leads to rapid cuts of fleeting childhood memories.

Projected at UCLA's Broad Art Center in the EDA, su/per/cut is an immersive two minute film that culminates my early memories. By scanning images from my childhood and stitching them together it enshrines that period of my life forever, acknowledging the importance of said memories while understanding the inevitable passage of time and the importance of growing up. Originally projected onto three surrounding walls through five projectors, these emotions are further intensified to overwhelm the viewer with nostalgic images and sounds.

Song: Supercut - Lorde
